Times X Table

Hello, and welcome. The purpose of this website is to generate multiplication tables based on the parameters that you, the user, provide. Before a table can be built, there are a few rules that must be followed. First, only integer numbers may be entered. Second, the "Min" feilds must be less than or equal to their respective "Max" feilds. Third, you must etner a value for each feild.

After you've entered valid entries, hit the "Build Table" button to build your table. Your table will appear in a tab with a tab heading that matches the values you entered, which will be formatted in the following way: [rowmin value, rowmax value],[colmin value, colmax value]. To close an single tab, click the "x" in the tab's upper right-hand corner. To close all tabs, click the "Close All Tabs" button.
